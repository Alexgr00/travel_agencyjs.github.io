Mumbai

Developed by the British in the eighteenth century, Bombay (as it was previously called) literally meant "A Good Bay." The city has a tangible sense of vibrancy with the hustle and bustle that embodies India. Its southern tip, which was the origin of the city, is filled with Gothic architecture including the magnificent Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, the Rajabai Clock Tower and the Taj Mahal Hotel.
